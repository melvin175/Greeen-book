Plants

Why love plants?

Why not? With all the varieties of tree's presenting their beauty to the human eyes independently scattered and beautifully arranged to heighten the mood stimulations of happy thoughts. A study looking at the most calming image found the combination of green plants and blue water to be the most attractive and calming.

Plants are healthy. They promote health and well-being as well as you getting to nurture something alive. It's so cool to plant something in the ground, fertilize, water, and care for it all while watching it grow and bloom. I love my plants. They help people with stress and anxiety, too.

My Work

I’ve always had this strange fascination with trees. They have this relentless consistency that is like gravity, all-powerful and unnoticed. Existing as a given. Trees grow. They seek water and sunlight without the burdens and insecurities of human thought. Their mission is pure.

Plants are also a central part of a wide variety of more modern art, such as arabesque, still life paintings etc. They have fascinated artists for years they’re in the art of the ancient Egyptians, and the Chinese. Photographing plants is a rewarding activity for any photographer. Well I don't know how it works for others but it is for me.
